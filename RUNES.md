# Rune Crafting Rules

### Rules
>Crafting Rules:
>- `Weekly` DC 15
>- 2 + `INT` mod attempts
>- Roll `DEX` + proficiency
>- Can make half as many attempts to roll `INT` + proficiency (being careful)
>- Can spend a week preparing to gain advantage on next week's rolls
